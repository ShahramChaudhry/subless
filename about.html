About SLess

Making subscription management simple and efficient for the UAE market

Our Mission

We believe that managing subscriptions shouldn't be complicated. SLess was born from the frustration of forgotten subscriptions draining budgets across the UAE.

Our mission is to help users regain control of their spending by providing a simple, intelligent platform that automatically tracks, manages, and optimizes their subscription expenses.
